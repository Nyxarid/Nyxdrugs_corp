A Revelation in the Checkout Line

Nyxdrugs Corp. was founded by visionary C.E.O. Bartholomew Nyx after a profound moment of anti-clarity while waiting to buy milk. He watched people fueled by caffeine and ambition, rushing to be better, faster, stronger. "But to what end?" he whispered to a carton of eggs. "What if the real luxury isn't success, but a temporary and amusing distraction?"
